Thank you for stopping by!

Dear friends,

Thank you for paying me a visit today. Friends and good people are always welcome to this website. Treats are encouraged, but by no means expected. Ok, if you feel that you must, please mail treats to my PO Box at 155 Catsville Road, Wilmington, DE 19806. Although there is definitely no reason to do this, feel free to give me Iams 9oz cans of Salmon flavored cat food. Chicken is also good, but Salmon is more savory and reminds me of being in the wild. Oh yeah, I am a house cat.

Before you leave today, let me share a bit of truth about cats. Although we are cute and seem like normal pets, in reality we are from Mars. That's right, we came from outer space. A long time ago someone noticed a pod laying in a field. They brought it home because it was so unusual. What an odd rock, they thought. It had black spots and whiskers sticking out from each side.

As the story goes, three days and four nights later, the family noticed a small furry paw stick out from the "rock." Then, another paw! Something was coming out of it! The shell broke open and out emerged a small furry black cat. He was strong, and wild, and had skills like no other animal on planet earth. Ok, ok, I made up this story. But, remember, cats are cool and you should love them all!

P.S. Iams Salmon flavor... don't forget.
