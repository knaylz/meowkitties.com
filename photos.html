Here's the real me!

I thought what better way to show off my cuteness than to post these pictures of my favorite things: 1.) my bed 2.) my strings! Here you can see me playing with two of my toys. Every day I nap from around 9am until 5pm when the humans get home. That is when they give me the toys. When they're here they give me lots of love and that's basically what I live for. I'm never afraid of strangers, in fact I get the pizza delivery person to pet me too!

The humans usually go to bed at 10 at night, and I go to bed at that time too, but only for one hour. Then, I wake up, eat and walk around most of the night. They aren't sure what I actually do, but are covinced I do a lot because I sleep so much during the day. Who could blame me? Life is great!

Now, where was I? Oh yes. Strings. Those glorious woven threads I long to get my paws on. Believe it or not my favorite string is a shoestring from a man's dress shoe. I think it's sturdy and provides a lot of flexibility when I grab it and try to destroy it. The other toys are more for show, but I play with them to make my humans happy. Really, any type of zip up or hoodie strings are my favorite. I love to attack them! It is specially fun when my humans are laying casually on the bed or sitting on a coach. I give them a few minutes to get comfy, and when they least expect it ... rawr attack attack!
